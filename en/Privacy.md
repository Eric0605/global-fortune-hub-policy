When you use AI-assisted features, limited contextual information related to your request may be transmitted to AI service providers for processing. AI-generated responses may be produced using third-party systems. We do not control the training data, internal logic, or outputs of those systems and cannot guarantee their accuracy or appropriateness.

Where supported by the Service, outputs may also be normalized into your locale or translated using third-party language or translation providers.

AI-generated outputs may be stored in the app, returned through our cloud functions, or saved in your journal if you choose to save them.

You should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information such as government identifiers, financial account numbers, or medical records unless you are comfortable with that information being processed through cloud systems.
